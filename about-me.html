>_ About Me   

Self-taught pentester and developer driven by curiosity and a constant need to understand how systems really work — and how they break. I focus on identifying vulnerabilities, exploiting real-world scenarios, and building tools that automate and simplify complex security tasks.

I don’t just look for vulnerabilities — I aim to understand the logic behind them.
